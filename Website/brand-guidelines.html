About This Document

This brand guidelines document defines the visual identity and communication standards for AZ Laundry Service. All marketing materials, digital assets, and communications should follow these guidelines to maintain a consistent, recognizable brand across every touchpoint.

AZ Laundry Service — Brand Guidelines 2
01
Our Brand

AZ Laundry Service is a pickup-and-delivery laundry service built for college students in the Tempe and Scottsdale area. We make laundry disappear so students can focus on what actually matters — studying, socializing, and surviving college. No subscriptions, no hassle, no trips to the laundromat.

Simple

No subscriptions. No long-term commitments. Text Dave, we pick it up, we bring it back. That's it.

Affordable

$1.50/lb with a $20 minimum. 50% off your first order. Built for a student budget.

Fast

48-hour turnaround. Free pickup and delivery. Your laundry comes back clean, folded, and ready.

Local

Arizona-based, ASU-adjacent, and run by real people. Not a faceless corporate laundry app.

Target Audience

ASU college students aged 18–24 in Tempe and Scottsdale, Arizona. Our brand voice speaks to them like a peer — casual, direct, and no-nonsense. We never sound corporate or overly polished.

Our logo features a maroon shield with a delivery van, laundry bag, washing machine porthole, Arizona sun rays, cactus, and stars. The wordmark reads "AZ LAUNDRY SERVICE" with the website URL. The logo is available in several configurations for different uses.

Primary Logo — Light Background

Full-color version for white or light surfaces. Default choice.

Primary Logo — Dark Background

White/gold text version for dark or maroon surfaces.

Icon Mark — Shield Only

Favicons, app icons, social avatars, small spaces.

Icon Mark — On Brand Maroon

Shield on the primary maroon background.

Preferred logo: Always use the full-color version on light backgrounds (top left) as the default. Only use the dark background version when the surface is dark or when contrast requires it. Use the shield icon only when space is too small for the full wordmark.

AZ Laundry Service — Brand Guidelines 5
04
Logo Don'ts

To protect the integrity of our brand, never modify the logo in any of the following ways. Always use the provided logo files as-is.

Don't stretch or distort
Don't use at low opacity
Don't use in grayscale
Don't rotate the logo
Don't add drop shadows
Don't place on busy backgrounds

Additional restrictions:

  • Don't rearrange the shield and wordmark elements
  • Don't change the logo colors or recolor any part
  • Don't add outlines, borders, or strokes to the logo
  • Don't place text or other logos overlapping the mark
  • Don't recreate or redraw the logo — always use the provided files
  • Don't use the logo as a pattern or repeating background

AZ Laundry Service — Brand Guidelines 8
07
Brand Voice
& Tone

We talk to students like a friend who happens to do their laundry. Casual, direct, and zero fluff. Short sentences. Real talk. No marketing buzzwords.

Casual
Like texting a friend
Direct
Get to the point
Honest
No hidden fees or tricks
Fun
Laundry doesn't have to suck

Do

  • Use short, punchy sentences
  • Talk like a peer, not a corporation
  • Use "you" and "your" — speak directly
  • Be specific with pricing and timelines
  • Use humor when it feels natural
  • Address real student pain points

Don't

  • Use corporate jargon or buzzwords
  • Over-explain or use filler words
  • Be vague about costs or process
  • Use exclamation points excessively
  • Say "we're passionate about laundry"
  • Sound like a generic startup pitch
Copy Examples
Write this "Your laundry pile is judging you. We'll make it disappear. $1.50/lb, 48 hours, done."
Not this "At AZ Laundry Service, we are passionate about providing exceptional laundry solutions for the modern student lifestyle."
Write this "Text Dave. We pick it up. You get it back clean. That's it."
Not this "Our seamless pickup and delivery process ensures a hassle-free experience from start to finish!"
